Local Tehachapi Honey

Pure, local honey crafted with care in the beautiful mountain community of Tehachapi, California. Newsom Honey Farm offers a natural product you can trust, harvested and prepared with pride from hive to jar.

At Newsom Honey Farm, we take pride in providing high-quality, locally produced honey from Tehachapi, California. Our honey is carefully harvested, processed, and packaged with attention to quality in every step.

We offer honey in a variety of sizes, from 1-pound jars to 40-pound containers, along with pure beeswax products. Whether you are looking for a thoughtful gift, something special for a welcome basket, or a delicious everyday pantry staple, our honey is a natural choice.

In addition to its rich flavor, honey has long been appreciated for its natural qualities, including helping soothe sore throats and supporting skin care. Our honey is raw, pure, and free from additives, giving you a product you can trust.

What sets Newsom Honey Farm apart is our commitment to quality and transparency. Our honey is harvested, processed, packaged, and sold by the same dedicated beekeeper, so we know exactly where it comes from.

Taste the difference that comes from local care, craftsmanship, and a passion for beekeeping.
