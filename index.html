Local Tehachapi Honey

At Newsom Honey Farm, we take pride in providing high-quality, locally produced honey from the beautiful mountain community of Tehachapi, California.

Our honey is carefully harvested, processed, and packaged with attention to quality in every step.

We offer honey in a variety of sizes, from 1-pound jars to 40-pound containers, along with pure beeswax products.

Whether you are looking for a thoughtful gift, something special for a welcome basket, or a delicious everyday pantry staple, our honey is a natural choice.

In addition to its rich flavor, honey has long been appreciated for its natural qualities, including helping soothe sore throats and supporting skin care.

Our honey is raw, pure, and free from additives, giving you a product you can trust.
